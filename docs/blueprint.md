# **App Name**: ShopX Elite

## Core Features:

- Intelligent Shop Dashboard: A high-fidelity dashboard aggregating sales, profit margins, pending payments, and stock values in real-time charts.
- Stock Intelligence AI Tool: Generative AI tool that uses reasoning to analyze past sales data and stock levels to forecast inventory needs and suggest restocking schedules.
- Advanced SKU & Inventory Vault: Centralized database for product tracking with expiry alerts, low-stock notifications, and full purchase-to-sale history.
- Dynamic GST Invoicing: Professional billing engine with multi-format export and integrated tax calculations for diverse payment methods.
- Digital Customer & Supplier Ledger: Manage outstanding balances and credit tracking with individual history logs for every business relationship.
- Accounting & Financial Reports: Automated generation of P&L statements, Trial Balance, and Cash Book data based on daily business activity.
- Granular Access Control: Secure multi-user management with specific role-based permissions for Owner, Manager, and Staff accounts.
- Secure Authentication & Onboarding: Modern login and signup interfaces featuring JWT-based session management and secure account registration workflows.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Electric Azure (#4285F4) evoking stability and high-tech precision. Background: Deep Midnight (#10131A) for a modern, focused work environment.
- Accent: Bright Turquoise (#2ED6E0) used for high-contrast indicators, CTAs, and positive financial trends.
- Font pairing: 'Space Grotesk' for technical-style headlines and 'Inter' for legible, high-density data tables and accounting entries.
- Numerical display: Uses 'Source Code Pro' for monospaced figures in ledgers and invoices to ensure column alignment.
- A structured, bento-grid style dashboard that prioritizes 'today's' metrics above fold with collapsible sidebar navigation.
- Micro-line iconography with consistent stroke weight to maintain a clinical, professional agency aesthetic.
- State transitions utilize subtle spring-based scaling for modular dashboard cards when updating real-time data.
